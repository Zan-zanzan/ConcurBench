    %% Main 函数入口

    end

    %% 子进程调用
    Task1 -->|CreateProcess| Process1[子进程1]

    Process1 -->|管道输出| Result[捕获输出]
    Process2 -->|管道输出| Result
    ProcessN -->|管道输出| Result

    %% 结果写入
    Result --> Writer[[ConcurrentResultWriter]]
    Writer -->|互斥写入| ResultFile[result.txt]
